About Decimal

Decimal Data Services are a market-leading provider of real-time sports data and analytics. While they had an established and feature-rich platform, their design language was in need of a refresh. Furthermore, their process for introducing new features often involved verbal briefs to developers without design references, leading to inefficient back-and-forth and wasted time.

My partnership with Decimal focused on refining their visual identity, streamlining their feature development process, and building a scalable design foundation.

Brand polish

A key initial step was to update Decimal's visual identity to better reflect their position as a market-leading data provider. This involved subtle but impactful updates to the logo, typography, and colour palette, applied across their on and offline marketing materials and customer portal. The introduction of illustration and animation helped to create a more sophisticated and dynamic brand expression.

Effective Design Process

To address challenges in the feature development process, I implemented a more structured approach to implementing new features. Working closely with the Head of Product and Dev Lead through dedicated workshops, I gathered and refined requirements. I translated these into clear wireframes, user flows, and mockups. This provided the development team with precise design references and a shared understanding of scope, significantly reducing ambiguity and improving efficiency.

About Zuntold

Zuntold offers a unique fiction-based mental health support platform for young people. Before our engagement, Zuntold had a functional but scrappy MVP which presented some user experience friction. While they had exciting ideas for new features, the details weren't fully documented or brought to life, making it challenging for their development team to accurately estimate build time and cost for future enhancements.   My partnership with Zuntold focused on providing strategic product design support to overcome these hurdles and prepare for growth.

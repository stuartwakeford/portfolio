I’m a Lead Product Designer with nearly 20 years experience working with start-ups, scale-ups and large enterprise.

I act as a trusted strategic partner for my clients, guiding them through the intricate process of bringing new products and services to market with a comprehensive, end-to-end product design skillset (read more on my services here). I often work in a fractional capacity, providing senior-level design leadership and expertise precisely when and where it's needed, offering flexibility and high-impact contributions without the overhead of a full-time executive.

Approach

Collaborative and hands-on, I embed myself in every aspect of the product development cycle, from initial discovery and strategy to execution and iteration. I thrive on working at speed, rapidly testing ideas and iterating to find the most effective solutions. A key part of my role is acting as a vital bridge between founders and the rest of the team - particularly engineers - using design as a powerful tool to communicate complex ideas clearly and facilitate efficient learning and development.

Career

I began my career working within reputable London design and digital agencies, delivering web and game experiences for large enterprises like Bloomberg, BP, KPMG and BT. Leveraging this experience I started, grew, and successfully exited my own ventures in the digital health space, including a boutique digital health agency and a health tracking app. Drawing on this rich experience, I currently act as a strategic design partner to ambitious founders, applying my extensive experience and insights to help them transform their innovative ideas into market-leading products and services.

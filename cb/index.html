Services

Charlie offers bespoke solutions for projects of all shapes and sizes.

Each project starts with a discovery call to discuss your needs, explore how Charlie can help and the level of service your project may require. These calls are free of charge, and there’s no obligation to proceed beyond this.

Charlie’s consultation service offers you the flexibility to seek expert advice at any point during your project, whether you're just starting out or need guidance on specific challenges as your project progresses.

The time can be used to discuss any interior design issues you may have, for example:

  • Optimising your space and room layout
  • Choosing colour schemes, paint colours, and material finishes
  • Selecting the right furniture and storage solutions
  • Window treatments, flooring and lighting
  • Styling and finishing touches

Prior to your meeting you’ll be asked to complete a detailed questionnaire. Based on this Charlie will research and prepare, ensuring you get the most from your time together during the consultation.

If you would like to move forward with additional design services the consultation cost will be deducted from the final fee.

Consultations are available in-person or via video call, depending on location. Travel costs are charged for in-person meetings beyond Brighton & Hove.

If you feel you need more comprehensive support, the Room Re-design package offers expert, personalised recommendations that you can implement yourself.

Designed for those who don’t require Full-Service Design —whether due to budget constraints or a desire for less intensive support— this package provides professional guidance without the added cost.

Charlie can assist with a single room or a collection of rooms, delivering a tailored plan that includes:

  • Colour schemes (with specific paint options)
  • Design finishes (key materials and where to use them)
  • Layout options (x3)
  • Furniture ideas (styles, brands, sizing)
  • Tips and tricks (expert hacks to finesse the space)

It is also possible to add on additional services should you need them along the way. For example furniture sourcing, bespoke cabinetry design, lighting plans, site visits and so on.

For clients that want a seamless transformation of their home or commercial space. This is a turnkey design service that manages every aspect of a project, from concepts to design details, through to project coordination and styling.

Experience

Over 18 years' experience working with agencies, large organisations and start-ups.

Agency

I began my career in London design agencies, climbing the ranks from Junior Designer to Design Director.

Founder

In 2013 I co-founded Prosper, a digital agency predominantly working with clients in the health space.

Idea to Exit

I co-founded Health Mapper in 2013, an app for tracking health conditions that would go on to front the App Store, get 25,000 users and be acquired by Doctor Care Anywhere in 2015.

IPO

As Head of Design at Doctor Care Anywhere I built and steered a design team that overhauled the brand, visual language and user experience of the entire product portfolio. DCA went public through IPO in 2020.

Design partner

I’m currently collaborating with clients of all shapes and sizes, using my skills and experience to bring great ideas to life.

Side-hustle

In 2019 I launched TEAMZ, a light-touch alternative to traditional Fantasy Footoball where users pick teams instead of players.

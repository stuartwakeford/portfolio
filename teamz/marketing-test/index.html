Each week you spend your kitty on a fresh set of teamz from around Europe. This makes picking much less of a chore and you won’t get stuck with the same crap selection from week-to-week.

Familiar Fantasy Scoring

Just like normal fantasy football, you'll earn points for goals scored, wins and clean sheets, and lose points for goals conceded and defeats.
Your points can be boosted by picking one team for double points and correctly predicting clean sheets.

Relegation and promotion every 4 rounds

Every four rounds there will be promotion and relegation battles as you try to climb through the leagues. One bad round won’t mean an end to your chances of glory for the year! League tables are capped at 20 players, so no more coming in 23,456th place.

Approach

Collaborative and hands-on, I embed myself in every aspect of the product development cycle, from initial discovery and strategy to execution and iteration. I like to work at speed, rapidly testing ideas and iterating to find the most effective solutions. A key part of my role is acting as a vital bridge between founders and the rest of the team - particularly engineers - using design as a powerful tool to communicate complex ideas clearly and provide a north star.

Career

I began my career working within reputable digital design agencies in London, delivering web and game experiences for large enterprises like Bloomberg, BP, KPMG and BT. Leveraging this experience I started, grew, and successfully exited my own ventures in the digital health space, including a digital health consultancy and health tracking app (see Health Mapper below).

In 2015 I was appointed Head of Design at healthcare scale-up Doctor Care Anywhere. This period saw me build and grow a design team responsible for a comprehensive product and markeing overhaul in the run up to an IPO.

Drawing on this rich experience, I currently act as a strategic design partner to founders and product owners, applying my extensive experience and insights to help them transform their ideas into market-leading products and services.
